        PPC Success Story

        Goal

        • → Improve PPC conversions
        • → Create campaign highlighting upcoming European Accessibility Legislation

        How

        • → Tweak PPC campaigns that were performing best
        • → Created EAA Keyword strategy, ads, and campaign

        Results

        • → 467 conversions
        • → 3 deals created, totaling $85,000 since July 2024

        I started managing our Google Ads PPC in July 2024 and finished in March 2025. UsableNet wasn’t seeing a ton of success with outsourcing our PPC endeavors, and we wanted to see if we could generate more leads if someone internally took over the PPC campaigns. In January of 2025 we launched the PPC campaign for the European Accessibility Act, an important web accessibility directive from the European Union.

        Our PPC conversion rate for the European Accessibility Act for Q1 2025 was over 7% and had 317 conversions. Since I started taking over our PPC efforts, UsableNet had 467 total conversions, 3 of which became created opportunities totaling $85,000 in pipeline.
